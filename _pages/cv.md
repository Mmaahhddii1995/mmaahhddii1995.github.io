## Research Statement

My research lies at the intersection of **security, privacy, and society**, with a focus on making strong privacy guarantees practical and deployable. I work on overcoming long-standing limitations in **mix networks**, particularly latency and scalability, which have historically prevented their adoption in real-world systems. My contributions are now considered part of the **state of the art in mixnet research** and aim to enable scalable, low-latency anonymous communication for everyday internet use.
